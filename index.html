About Me

Hello! I'm IAN RAVAGO, "The Augmented Developer." My journey into the world of technology is driven by an insatiable curiosity – I'm a true Curious Explorer at heart.

I specialize in leveraging cutting-edge AI tools like Gemini and VS Copilot to accelerate development, brainstorm innovative solutions, and bring complex ideas to life. My passion lies at the intersection of human ingenuity and artificial intelligence, creating applications that are not only functional but also push the boundaries of what's possible.

This portfolio showcases my "AI-Driven Creations" – projects where AI has been a key collaborator. I believe in making technology accessible and enjoy the process of learning and building in this rapidly evolving landscape. This very site, in fact, was co-created with AI, a testament to the collaborative power I champion.
